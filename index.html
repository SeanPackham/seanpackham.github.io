My story

My passion for card games started when I discovered Pokemon and Magic: The Gathering. To this day, I still love the excitement of brewing decks and pulling off combos.

Growing up, I fondly remember marathon board game sessions that made days flyby, and the neighbourhood huddled around a computer taking turns to play Might and Magic.

My uncle networked their house with the neighbours and we played roleplaying and strategy video games until the images burned into our retinas.

Today, I'm still hooked to RPGs, I keep coming back to MTG (thank you Arena) and have discovered new digital favourites like Slay the Spire and Into the Breach.

I learned to code in high school to work out how to make games. I went on to study computer science, game development and AI at university.

Many years later and I've worked in the games industry with AAA and indie studios, lectured game design and helped grow fast-paced startups across the globe.

But I'm not done yet...

Why I'm building CardTurn

I'm channelling my passion for games with everything I've learnt into building CardTurn.

CardTurn will empower game designers and developers to quickly and easily design, develop, play and share card, board and turn-based games.

I want to inspire new generations of game makers and players to create their own adventures.

I want to accelerate industry experts to expand their creativity and output.
